# AI Professional Interview Simulator Requirements Document
\n## 1. Application Overview\n**Application Name:** AI Professional Interview Simulator
\n**Application Description:** An intelligent interview simulation system that conducts realistic, adaptive professional interviews by combining expertise from senior HR, technical leadership, behavioral psychology, and career coaching perspectives. The system simulates multi-role panel interviews with dynamic difficulty adjustment and comprehensive feedback mechanisms.

## 2. Core Functionality

### 2.1 Multi-Role Interview Simulation
- AI acts as multiple interviewers simultaneously:\n  - HR Manager: Focuses on cultural fit, communication, and organizational alignment
  - Technical Lead: Evaluates technical knowledge and problem-solving skills
  - Behavioral Coach: Assesses soft skills, emotional intelligence, and teamwork
  - Domain Expert: Provides industry-specific technical challenges
- Each role asks domain-specific questions and provides unique perspectives
- Simulates realistic panel interview dynamics

### 2.2 Interview Structure
- Professional greeting and interview structure explanation
- Multi-dimensional assessment covering:
  - Core technical skills evaluation
  - Problem-solving and analytical thinking
  - Behavioral competencies (teamwork, leadership, adaptability)
  - Situational judgment and real-world scenarios
  - Cultural fit and communication skills
  - Emotional intelligence assessment
\n### 2.3 Adaptive Questioning System
- One question at a time delivery with natural conversational flow
- Real-time difficulty adjustment based on candidate responses:\n  - If candidate excels: Questions become progressively harder
  - If candidate struggles: System provides hints or simplifies questions
- Intelligent follow-up questions for deeper probing
- Dynamic coverage across multiple domains (technical, behavioral, situational)
- Realistic pauses and conversational timing to encourage thoughtful responses

### 2.4 Multi-Mode Interview Formats
- **Technical Interview Mode:** Coding challenges, system design, technical problem-solving
- **Behavioral Interview Mode:** STAR method questions, past experience evaluation
- **Situational Interview Mode:** Real-world scenario-based judgment questions
- **Quick-Fire Knowledge Mode:** Rapid-response technical or domain knowledge questions
- **Comprehensive Mixed Mode:** Rotating between all question types
- **Custom Configuration Mode:** Tailored interview focus areas

### 2.5 Detailed Evaluation System
\n#### Per-Answer Feedback:\n- Multi-dimensional scoring (1-10 scale) for each response:\n  - Technical knowledge accuracy
  - Problem-solving approach
  - Communication clarity
  - Behavioral competencies
  - Cultural fit indicators
- Constructive assessment highlighting strengths and improvement areas
- Example model answers for reference
- Actionable suggestions for better responses
- Option to provide hints and allow answer revision with re-evaluation

#### Final Comprehensive Evaluation:
- Overall scores across all dimensions with visual progress bars
- Detailed strengths and weaknesses summary
- Personalized improvement plan:\n  - 1-week intensive prep plan
  - 1-month comprehensive development plan
- Specific learning resources:\n  - Recommended courses and books
  - Practice exercises and mock scenarios
  - Industry-specific study materials
- Advice and strategies for real interviews
\n### 2.6 Behavioral & Situational Intelligence
- STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) framework for behavioral questions
- Real-world scenario presentation for situational judgment
- Evaluation of:\n  - Emotional intelligence
  - Teamwork and collaboration skills
  - Leadership qualities and potential
  - Conflict resolution abilities
  - Adaptability and resilience\n
### 2.7 Simulated Pressure & Challenge Mode
- Time-limited problem-solving questions
- High-pressure scenario simulation
- Stress-test behavioral responses with difficult situations
- Prepares candidates for real high-stakes interviews

### 2.8 Candidate Profile Configuration
- Name input\n- Experience level specification (Junior/Mid/Senior/Executive)\n- Skills listing (technical and soft skills)
- Desired role definition
- Career level selection\n- Job domain and industry customization
- Previous interview experience tracking

### 2.9 Industry-Specific Customization
- Questions adjusted based on:\n  - Target role requirements
  - Experience level
  - Industry domain (tech, finance, healthcare, etc.)
- Domain-specific case studies\n- Industry-relevant coding challenges or technical problems
- Sector-specific behavioral scenarios

### 2.10 Interactive & Iterative Learning
- Hint system when candidate struggles
- Answer revision and re-evaluation capability
- Multi-round interview simulation:\n  - Initial screening round
  - Technical deep-dive round
  - HR and cultural fit round
  - Final executive round
- Continuous improvement tracking across multiple sessions
- Progress visualization showing skill development over time

## 3. Key Features
- Professional, welcoming tone maintenance throughout
- Realistic interview simulation with human-like interaction
- Actionable, constructive feedback focused on growth
- Dynamic difficulty scaling for optimal challenge level
- Multi-role interviewer perspective integration
- Engagement-focused interaction design
- Comprehensive skill assessment across technical and soft skills
- Personalized learning path generation
- Session history and progress tracking

## 4. Design Style
- **Color Scheme:** Professional blue (#2C5F8D) as primary color, paired with neutral gray (#F5F7FA) background and accent orange (#FF8C42) for highlights, creating a trustworthy and focused atmosphere
- **Layout:** Clean card-based layout with clear separation between interviewer panel display, question area, input area, feedback section, and scoring dashboard
- **Visual Details:** Subtle shadows for depth, rounded corners (8px) for modern feel, progress indicator showing interview stages and completion percentage, role badges for different interviewer personas
- **Typography:** Clear hierarchy with bold headings for questions, regular weight for body text, highlighted feedback sections with color-coded scoring indicators
- **Interactive Elements:** Smooth transitions between questions, typing indicators during AI response generation, score visualization with animated progress bars, hint button with subtle pulse effect, timer display for time-limited questions
